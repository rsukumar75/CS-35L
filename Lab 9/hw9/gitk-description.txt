Description of gitk subwindows.

Upper Half of screen.

Top Left: This subwindow shows the commit history with respect to the branches.
It provides a graphical representation of commits and merges.

Top Middle: This subwindow shows the information of the specific commit's author.

Top Right: This subwindow shows the timestamp of the corresponding action.

Bottom half of screen.

Bottom Left: This subwindow shows the changelog of the selected commit.
It also shows actual file changes in unified diff format.

Bottom Right: This subwindow shows comments (Usually the files impacted by the commit)
